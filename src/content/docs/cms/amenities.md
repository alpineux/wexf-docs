# Amenities

## What This Is

This is a content collection that stores information about amenities (features and services) for your business or property. Think of it like a digital filing cabinet that holds details about all the special features you offer.

## What It Does

The Amenities collection organizes and manages information about the various features, services, or perks you provide. This could include things like Wi-Fi, parking, pools, gyms, or other facilities. When visitors browse your website, they can see what amenities are available.

## Key Details

- **Collection name**: Amenities
- **URL pattern**: Content appears at `/amenities` on your website
- **Current items**: 42 amenities are stored in this collection
- **Content type**: Individual amenity (like "Free Wi-Fi" or "Swimming Pool")

## Things to Know

> **Note**: This collection doesn't currently have any custom fields set up. This means you can add amenity items, but there's no structure for storing specific details about each one.

You might want to consider adding fields like:
- Amenity name or title
- Description of what it includes
- Photos or icons
- Whether it's free or has additional costs
- Location or availability hours

Having 42 amenities already suggests this collection is actively used, so adding proper fields would help organize and display this information more effectively on your website.
