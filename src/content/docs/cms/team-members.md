# Team Members

## What This Is

This is your website's team directory - a collection that stores information about all the people who work at your company. Think of it like a digital staff roster that displays on your website.

## What It Does

This collection powers your team or about us page, showing visitors who works at your company. Each team member gets their own profile with details like their name, job title, photo, and background information. The collection is set up at the URL path `/team-members`.

## Key Details

- **Collection name**: Team Members
- **Individual items called**: Team Member  
- **Website URL**: Your site's team pages will appear at `/team-members/[person-name]`
- **Current team size**: 10 people are currently listed

## Things to Know

This collection is currently empty of field definitions, which means it's been set up but the specific information fields (like name, photo, job title) haven't been configured yet. You'll need to add fields to start building out individual team member profiles.

Once fields are added, this collection will automatically create individual pages for each team member that visitors can browse through. It's perfect for helping customers and clients put faces to names at your company.
